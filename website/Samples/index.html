E.g.,

                    -out DependencyFinder-diff-1.2.0-1.2.1.xml

    C:\> OOMetrics -xml -project-name "Dependency Finder" -out DependencyFinder-metrics-1.2.1 DependencyFinder.jar

                       -out DependencyFinder-metrics-1.2.1.html
