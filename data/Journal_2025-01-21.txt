Wrote a JSON printer for dependency cycles.

These cycles:

    a
        --> b
            --> a
    a
        --> b
            --> c
                --> a

Are the following JSON document:

    [
        [
            {"type": "package", "name": "a"},
            {"type": "package", "name": "b"}
        ],
        [
            {"type": "package", "name": "a"},
            {"type": "package", "name": "b"},
            {"type": "package", "name": "c"}
        ]
    ]
